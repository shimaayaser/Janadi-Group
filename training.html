Janadi Group organizes training activities tailored to the specific needs of its clients. Our programs cover a wide range of disciplines including entrepreneurship, strategy, organization, management, finance, and more. We offer training workshops adapted to the type and size of the organization: project leaders, VSEs, SMEs, GEs, and institutions. Our firm provides two types of programs:

Pre-Established Programs

Public training courses designed in advance with specific dates and locations.

Contract Programs

Customized to meet the client’s needs while considering their specific requirements and expectations.

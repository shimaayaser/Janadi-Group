About Janadi Group

Founded in 2019, Janadi Group is a specialized consulting firm dedicated to empowering entrepreneurs, startups, SMEs, and project developers. We provide comprehensive support throughout every stage of the business lifecycle, offering strategic, organizational, financial, and commercial consulting services tailored to our clients’ needs.

Our training and development programs are delivered by seasoned experts with professional qualifications, extensive hands-on experience, and advanced scientific knowledge. At Janadi Group, we are committed to driving growth, fostering innovation, and helping businesses unlock their full potential.
